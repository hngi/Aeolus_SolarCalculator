WHY SOLAR POWER?

To many, investing in solar power is a difficult decision and not one that can be easily decided. There are many benefit to solar power and it important to have good knowledge of it. Our site is designed to help you calculate how much power is needed to effectively power your home or business.

BASICS ABOUT GOING SOLAR

HOW DO SOLAR PANEL WORK?

Solar panels are made of highly excitable, conductive materials. when the sun ray's rays hit the solar panels, the reaction creates direct current (DC) electricity. Since most homes and businesses uses alternating current (AC), your solar generated DC energy will pass through an inverter to become AC electricity.

HOW WILL SWITCHING TO SOLAR ENERGY HELP ME BUDGET BETTER?

When you install solar panels on your property, you will be generating more electricity than you use from the utiliy company at the price of free. This helps to reduce stress on budget.

HOW DOES SOLAR ENERGY BENEFIT THE ENVIRONMENT?

Solar power is a renewable resources, which means there's so much of it freely available we never have to dig for coal or refine other toxic energy sources just to keep the lights on.
